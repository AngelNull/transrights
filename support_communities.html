Support & Community

This page is dedicated to listing charities that the support transgender* community, and online trans* safe-spaces.

Charities

Charity Name Link Description
MermaidsUKhttps://mermaidsuk.org.uk/Specialising in support for the transgender* community,
Mermaids provides information and guidance for parents of
transgender children, as well as young people themselves.
StonewallUKhttps://www.stonewall.org.uk/Stonewall provides information, support and more to the 
LGBTQ+ community as a whole, and has a wide range of
information.
WeExisthttps://www.weexist.co.uk/Established in 2020, We Exist is a trans* led organisation that wants to provide more spaces for trans* people to platform their work, their ideas and discuss issues affecting the community.

Online Communities

Please note: whilst these communities have been listed here, I cannot personally vouch for the interactions you may have with members or staff, and should enter with a level of caution.

Charity Name Link Description
LGBTQ+ Hangouthttps://www.discord.gg/prideLGBTQ+ Hangout is a server for members or allies of the LGBTQ+
community to hangout together.
Gaymershttps://www.discord.gg/gaymersGaymers is a server for LGBTQ+ gamers. Providing spaces
for members to talk, play games together or get some advice.
r/TransgenderUKhttps://www.reddit.com/r/transgenderukr/TransgenderUK is a community on Reddit where you can talk about
UK politics regarding transgender* laws and policies, get advice on certain
topics and more.
r/Transhttps://www.reddit.com/r/transr/Trans is a global community where you can see and post pictures of yourself,
get advice and participate in discussions, and much more.
